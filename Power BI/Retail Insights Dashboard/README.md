#  Superstore Sales & Profit Insights Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze Superstore sales performance from **2014–2017**. The dashboard provides insights into sales, profit, customer segments, regional performance, and product categories through dynamic visualizations and filters.

---

## Overview

This dashboard helps business users monitor key sales metrics, identify profitable categories, evaluate regional performance, and explore customer purchasing patterns. Interactive slicers allow users to analyze data across different dates, regions, shipping modes, product categories, and customer segments.

---

## Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards for key business metrics
- Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering
- Sales trend analysis over time
- Category-wise sales comparison
- Regional sales analysis
- Customer segment distribution
- Top-selling products analysis
- Profit by category
- Profit by sub-category

## Key Insights

- Technology generated the highest overall profit among product categories.
- The West region recorded the highest sales performance.
- Consumer customers contributed the largest share of total sales.
- Sales showed steady growth during the analyzed period (2014–2017).
- A small number of products accounted for a significant portion of total sales.
- Interactive filters enable detailed analysis across regions, categories, shipping modes, and customer segments.
