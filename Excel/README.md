
# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive **Microsoft Excel dashboard** designed to analyze e-commerce sales performance, profitability, and product trends. The dashboard provides business insights through dynamic charts, KPI cards, and interactive filters for better decision-making.

---

## Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance by tracking key business metrics, category-wise profitability, monthly sales trends, and top-performing products. Interactive filters allow users to analyze data across different years, regions, and customer segments.

---

## Dashboard Features

- Executive KPI cards
- Year-over-Year (YoY) growth indicators
- Monthly Sales & Profit analysis
- Category-wise Profit analysis
- Category-wise Sales distribution
- State-wise Sales visualization
- Top-performing Sub-Categories
- Interactive dashboard filters

## Key Insights

- Sales exceeded **$2.29M** with an overall **12.47% profit margin**.
- Technology generated the highest category profit, followed by Office Supplies.
- Phones and Chairs ranked among the top-performing product sub-categories.
- Monthly sales and profit showed stronger performance during the later months of the year.
- Interactive filters enable comparison across years, regions, and customer segments for flexible business analysis.
